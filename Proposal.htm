Project Proposal

What is your website about?
My website is going to be a showcase of photographs I have taken during my trips.

What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)
My website is important because as self-taught photographer, I want to show people that photography doesn't always need the best equipment. More than anything, the angle makes up 90% of the shot.

Who is your target audience set?
Target audience are other amateur photographers or admirers of simple photography.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
I plan to include a homepage with a three button navigation system (subject to change of course). The three buttons will be my photographs, where I input a map where I then tag another page to certain areas of the map where I have taken pictures on those pages. My second button will be a site map where you can go to any page on the webpage from there.And then finally, a contact me pages for future questions from my target audience.
